About Us

Dedicated to creating lasting impact through compassion, transparency, and sustainable solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, SONVI NGO began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between privilege and poverty. What started as a small group of dedicated volunteers has grown into a movement impacting thousands of lives across multiple states.

Our approach combines grassroots community engagement with strategic partnerships, ensuring that our programs create sustainable impact. From organizing organ donation awareness camps to running educational mentorship programs, we work tirelessly to build hope and dignity for the underprivileged.

What Drives Us

Our Mission

To empower communities through education, healthcare awareness, and social welfare initiatives, creating a society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Vision

A world where compassion knows no boundaries, and every individual has access to the resources they need to live a dignified and fulfilling life.

Core Values

Integrity, Compassion, Transparency, and Sustainability guide every action we take and every program we implement.

Why Education Matters

Education is the foundation upon which we build our future. It equips individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to break the cycle of poverty and contribute meaningfully to society.

At SONVI, we believe every child deserves access to quality learning resources, regardless of their economic background.

  • Reduces poverty and increases economic growth
  • Promotes gender equality and empowers women
  • Fosters critical thinking and decision making

Our Initiatives

Comprehensive support systems designed to remove barriers to education.

Scholarship Program

Financial aid for meritorious students from low-income families to cover tuition and college fees.

Learning Materials

Distribution of textbooks, stationery, uniforms, and digital devices to under-resourced schools.

Mentorship & Coaching

Connecting students with mentors for career guidance, soft skills training, and personal development.

How It Works

Transparent and efficient process to ensure aid reaches the right hands.

01

Application

Students or guardians submit applications with necessary documentation.

Our team conducts background checks and home visits to verify needs.

03

Eligible candidates are selected based on need and academic potential.

04

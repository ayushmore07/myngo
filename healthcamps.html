Healthcare at Your Doorstep

Bridging the gap between rural communities and quality medical services through free health camps and awareness drives.

Medical Outreach Program

Access to healthcare is a fundamental right, yet millions in rural India go untreated due to lack of infrastructure and awareness. SONVI's Health Camp initiative brings doctors, medicines, and diagnostic tools directly to underserved villages.

We collaborate with district hospitals and private practitioners to organize free check-up camps focusing on preventative care and early diagnosis.

Our Focus Areas

Comprehensive care for all age groups.

General Health

Routine check-ups, BP & Sugar monitoring, and distribution of basic medicines for common ailments.

Eye & Vision

Vision testing, distribution of free spectacles, and cataract detection camps for the elderly.

Dental Hygiene

Dental check-ups, cleaning, and awareness sessions on oral hygiene for school children.

Women & Child

Prenatal care, nutrition guidance, and vaccination drives for mothers and infants.

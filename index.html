About Us

Building a Better Tomorrow, Together

Since 2018, SONVI NGO has been at the forefront of social change, touching thousands of lives through our dedicated programs and unwavering commitment.

6+
Years of Impact

Creating Lasting Change Through Compassion

SONVI NGO was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where every individual has access to healthcare, education, and dignity in their golden years.

Our approach combines grassroots community engagement with strategic partnerships, ensuring that our programs create sustainable impact. From organizing organ donation awareness camps to running educational mentorship programs, we work tirelessly to bridge gaps and build hope.

Our Programs

Initiatives That Transform Lives

Our carefully designed programs address critical needs in healthcare, education, and elderly care, creating meaningful impact in communities.

Saving lives through comprehensive awareness campaigns, donor registration drives, and family counseling. We bridge the gap between donors and recipients.

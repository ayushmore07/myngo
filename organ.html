What is Organ Donation?

Organ donation is the process of giving an organ or a part of an organ to be transplanted into another person. It is a noble act that offers the greatest gift of all: the gift of life.

A single donor can save up to eight lives and enhance the lives of over 75 people through tissue donation.

  • Gives patients a second chance at life
  • Creates a lasting legacy of compassion
  • Supported by all major religions

Myths vs Facts

Myth

Doctors won't try to save my life if they know I'm a donor.

Fact

Emergency doctors prioritize saving your life. The transplant team is entirely separate.

Myth

I'm too old to donate.

Fact

There is no age limit. Organs are evaluated for health, not the donor's age.

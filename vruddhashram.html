Providing a safe, loving home for our elders. Ensuring they live their golden years with respect, care, and companionship.

Our Vruddhashram

A Vruddhashram is more than just a shelter; it is a sanctuary for those who have been abandoned or neglected in their twilight years. At SONVI, we provide a home where elders are treated as family.

We believe that every individual deserves to age with dignity. Our mission is to fill their lives with joy, health, and the warmth of a community that cares.

  • Safe, hygienic accommodation
  • Nutritious meals tailored to health needs
  • Regular medical check-ups and counseling
